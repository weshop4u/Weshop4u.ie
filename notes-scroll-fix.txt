Scroll issue diagnosis:

The AdminDesktopLayout wraps children in a ScrollView (line 146).
Inside that, ProductPricesContent uses a FlatList (line 429).

Problem: Nested scrollable views. The outer ScrollView in AdminDesktopLayout 
and the inner FlatList in ProductPricesContent create a conflict on web.
On web, the browser's wheel event may be captured by the wrong scroll container,
or the FlatList's scroll may conflict with the outer ScrollView.

The ScreenContainer also adds another layer of Views.

Solution: For the Product Prices page, the FlatList should be the primary scroller.
The outer ScrollView in AdminDesktopLayout should not wrap this page, OR
we should replace FlatList with a simple .map() inside the outer ScrollView.

Best approach: Replace the FlatList with a regular View + .map() since the outer
AdminDesktopLayout already provides a ScrollView. The FlatList inside a ScrollView
is the classic nested scroll conflict.
