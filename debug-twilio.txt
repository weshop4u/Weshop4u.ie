TWILIO ERROR 60238 ANALYSIS
===========================
Error: "Verification Creation Attempt blocked by Twilio"
Code: 60238
Status: 403

Possible Causes (from Twilio docs):
1. Your request has been blocked because your upgrade request is going through a review process
2. Your request has been blocked because you are attempting to send to Iran, Syria or Cuba

This is NOT a Fraud Guard issue (that would be 60410 or 60605).
This is an ACCOUNT-LEVEL block — the Twilio account upgrade is still being reviewed.

Safe List won't help here because the block is at the account level, not the Fraud Guard level.

Solution: The user needs to wait for Twilio to complete their account review (up to 72 hours),
OR contact Twilio support to expedite the review.

Alternative: Implement a dev/testing bypass that skips OTP verification in development mode.
