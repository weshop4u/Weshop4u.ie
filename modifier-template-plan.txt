MODIFIER TEMPLATE SYSTEM PLAN
==============================

Current state:
- modifierGroups table: linked to productId (per-product only)
- modifiers table: linked to groupId
- modifierGroups has: id, productId, name, type, required, minSelections, maxSelections, sortOrder
- modifiers has: id, groupId, name, price, isDefault, isActive, sortOrder
- productCategories table: id, name, slug, description, icon, ageRestricted, availabilitySchedule, sortOrder

New tables needed:
1. modifier_templates - standalone reusable template definitions (not linked to any product)
   - id, name, type (single/multi), required, minSelections, maxSelections, createdAt
   
2. modifier_template_options - options within a template
   - id, templateId, name, price, isDefault, sortOrder, createdAt

3. category_modifier_templates - link templates to categories (auto-inherit)
   - id, categoryId, templateId, sortOrder, createdAt

4. product_modifier_templates - link templates to individual products (manual pick)
   - id, productId, templateId, sortOrder, createdAt

5. product_template_exclusions - opt out of category-level template for specific product
   - id, productId, templateId, createdAt

Approach for getForProduct:
1. Get product's categoryId
2. Get category-level templates (from category_modifier_templates)
3. Get product-level templates (from product_modifier_templates)
4. Get exclusions (from product_template_exclusions) - remove excluded category templates
5. Get custom per-product modifier groups (existing modifierGroups table)
6. Merge all together and return

Customer sees: all modifier groups merged, doesn't know the source
